Our Return Policy

At Enimito, your satisfaction is our priority. We offer a 7-day hassle-free return policy on all products. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return it for a full refund or exchange.

To be eligible for a return, your item must be:

  • In the same condition as received
  • In original packaging with all tags attached
  • Unused and undamaged
  • Returned within 7 days of delivery

Items Not Eligible for Return

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Items damaged due to misuse or negligence
  • Products without original packaging
  • Items purchased on clearance or final sale
  • Custom or personalized orders

Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we will process your refund. Refunds are issued via:

  • Cash on Return: If you visit our store
  • bKash/Nagad: Mobile banking transfer within 3-5 days
  • Bank Transfer: Direct bank transfer for large amounts

Shipping costs for returns are borne by the customer unless the item was defective or incorrect.
