Our Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not happy with your order, you can return it within 7 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

No questions asked. No restocking fees.

Return Conditions

  • Item must be in original condition and packaging
  • All tags, accessories, and manuals must be included
  • Return request must be made within 7 days of delivery
  • Custom or personalized items cannot be returned
  • Items damaged due to misuse are not eligible

How to Return

1 Contact us via email or WhatsApp within 7 days

2 We will send you a return authorization

3 Pack the item securely in original packaging

4 Send it back using our courier partner

5 Refund processed within 3-5 business days after we receive the item

Refund Method

  • Cash on Delivery orders: Refund via bKash or bank transfer
  • Online payments: Refund to original payment method
  • Processing time: 3-5 business days after we receive the returned item
