Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering from Enimito

How long does delivery take?

We offer 24-hour delivery across Kushtia and surrounding areas. For other districts in Bangladesh, delivery typically takes 2-3 business days. All orders are dispatched the same day they are placed.

What is your return policy?

We offer a hassle-free 7-day return policy. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return any item within 7 days of delivery for a full refund. The product must be in its original condition with all packaging intact.

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept Cash on Delivery (COD) for all orders across Bangladesh. Online payment options including bKash, Nagad, and card payments will be available soon.

Are your products authentic?

Yes! All our products are 100% authentic and fully imported from Japan, China, and certified international suppliers. We never sell counterfeit or replica items. Every product comes with its original packaging and certification where applicable.
