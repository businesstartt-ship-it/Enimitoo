Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering from Enimito

How long does delivery take?

We offer 24-hour delivery within Kushtia city and 2-3 days for other districts in Bangladesh. All orders are processed immediately and shipped via our trusted courier partners.

What is your return policy?

We offer a hassle-free 7-day return policy. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return the item in its original condition within 7 days for a full refund or exchange.

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept Cash on Delivery (COD) nationwide. Online payment options including bKash, Nagad, and card payments will be available soon.

Are your products authentic?

Yes! All our products are 100% authentic and fully imported from Japan, China, and certified international suppliers. We never sell counterfeit items.
