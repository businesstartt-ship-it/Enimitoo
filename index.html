About Enimito

Enimito was born from a deep love for Japanese anime culture and the art of handcrafted collectibles. Founded in 2022 by Udoy, we started as a small passion project in Kushtia, Bangladesh, and have grown into a trusted destination for anime enthusiasts across the country.

Every item in our collection is carefully selected and handcrafted with precision. From stunning anime figures that capture the essence of beloved characters, to authentic katanas forged with traditional techniques, to exclusive Hotwheels collaborations and intricate Demon Slayer merchandise — we ensure only the finest quality reaches your hands.

Our mission is simple: to bring the magic of anime culture to every fan's doorstep. With over 4,000 successful orders, we take pride in our 24-hour delivery promise, hassle-free 7-day returns, and our commitment to sourcing fully imported, authentic products.

Whether you're a seasoned collector or just beginning your journey, Enimito is here to make your anime dreams a reality.
