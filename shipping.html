Shipping Information

Fast, reliable delivery across Bangladesh

Delivery Timeframes

  • Kushtia City: Same day or within 24 hours
  • Khulna Division: 1-2 business days
  • Dhaka & Major Cities: 2-3 business days
  • Other Districts: 3-5 business days

Express 24-hour delivery available for select products in Kushtia area.

Shipping Costs

  • Orders over BDT 2,000: FREE shipping
  • Orders under BDT 2,000: BDT 100-150 depending on location
  • Cash on Delivery: Available nationwide (BDT 50 extra)

Packaging

All items are carefully packaged with protective materials to ensure they arrive in perfect condition. Fragile items like figures and katanas receive extra bubble wrap and foam padding.

Delivery Areas

We deliver to all 64 districts of Bangladesh through our courier partners. Remote areas may take an additional 1-2 days.
