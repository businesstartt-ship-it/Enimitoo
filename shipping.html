Shipping Information

Fast, reliable delivery across Bangladesh

Delivery Timeframes

We pride ourselves on fast delivery. Here are our estimated delivery times:

  • Kushtia & Surrounding Areas: Same day or within 24 hours
  • Khulna Division: 1-2 business days
  • Dhaka, Chittagong, Rajshahi: 2-3 business days
  • Other Districts: 3-5 business days

Packaging

All orders are carefully packaged to ensure your collectibles arrive in perfect condition:

  • Bubble wrap protection for fragile items
  • Sturdy cardboard boxes for figures and katanas
  • Waterproof packaging for all shipments
  • Original product packaging preserved whenever possible

Shipping Partners

We work with trusted courier services across Bangladesh including:

  • Sundarban Courier
  • SA Paribahan
  • RedX
  • Pathao (for express delivery)

Tracking information will be provided via SMS once your order is dispatched.

Delivery Areas

We deliver to all districts of Bangladesh. For remote areas, delivery may take an additional 1-2 days. Free delivery is available for orders over BDT 5,000 within Kushtia city limits.
